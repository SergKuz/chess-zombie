Chess vs Zombies

Move your King to the opposite side!
Turn: 1
Zombies: 0
Pieces: 7

Goal: Move your King to the top row while avoiding zombies

Controls:

- Click on a piece to select it, then click on a highlighted square to move

- After your move, zombies will automatically move and new ones may appear

- Most zombies are pawns, but beware special zombies (knights, bishops, rooks)

- Be strategic with each move as the game gets progressively harder
